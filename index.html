Manage your photo data

Your photo knows where you’ve been, what your camera is and ...

Get your photo data

Metapho is a simple and clean viewer for photo metadata such as file size, camera model, ISO, shutter speed, GPS coordinates and more.

Control your privacy

If you care about privacy before sharing your photo to public, erasing all metadata from a photo is easily done by Remove Metadata feature. It’s non–destructive operation so that you can restore metadata later.

Use directly in Photos Library

The new iOS 8 app extension let you check and remove metadata right inside iOS built–in Photos app.
