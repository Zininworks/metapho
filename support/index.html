FAQ

How can I unlock premium features?

When you’re seeing metadata of a photo, tapping a button with lock icon brings up in–app store. Single “Unlock Everything” in–app purchase unlocks everything.

Where can I find Metapho Action?

When you’re sharing an image via iOS share sheet, the bottom row of icons is where action extensions are. Tap the right most “More” icon and turn on Metapho.

Where’s Photo Edit Extension?

In the Photos app, tap a photo > Edit > Circle with dots button for 3rd party edit extension > More > turn on Metapho.

How to remove metadata permanently?

Tap Safe Share button—share button with dotted line, then select “Share without Metadata” to show iOS share sheet, and then tap “Save Image” icon from bottom row. A new image without metadata will be added to your Photos Libarary.

Does Metapho support iCloud?

Metapho is built upon the new Photos framework, works great with iCloud Photo Library(CPL). Every change syncs to CPL and will be available on devices with CPL enabled.

Does Metapho support dynamic type?

Ok. Not FAQ. Sadly this question is never asked. But yes. It took meticulous effort to make the app look good in any text size.
